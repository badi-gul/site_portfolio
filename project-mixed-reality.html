Project Overview

Our Mixed Reality division specializes in creating immersive experiences that blend digital elements with the physical world. From augmented reality activations to spatial computing experiences, we push the boundaries of what's possible when virtual and real environments converge.

Featured Projects

Urban Garden: AR Public Installation

We transformed a city plaza into an interactive digital garden using AR technology. Visitors could use their smartphones to reveal virtual plant life growing throughout the space, interact with digital creatures, and witness natural processes like growth and pollination visualized through CGI.

  • Augmented Reality
  • Public Installation
  • Interactive Design

FutureShop: Retail AR Experience

For a leading fashion retailer, we developed an AR showroom that allows customers to visualize products in their own space, try on virtual items through AR mirrors, and explore products with interactive 3D features that reveal manufacturing details and material information.

  • Retail Innovation
  • Product Visualization
  • Interactive Mirrors

TimePortal: Museum Spatial Experience

We created a spatial computing experience for a history museum that allows visitors to step into recreated historical environments. Using advanced headsets, visitors can interact with virtual artifacts, witness historical events, and engage with AI reconstructions of historical figures.

  • Spatial Computing
  • Historical Recreation
  • Interactive Learning
